3. The President (Giant Sequoia)

The President is one of the largest by volume and is a bit shorter than General Sherman but still one of the largest trees by girth.

  • Height - 75.3m
  • Diameter - 8.4m
  • Species - Sequoiadendron giganteum
  • Location - Sequoia National Park, California, USA

2. Hyperion (Coast Redwood)

Hyperion is the tallest tree in the world, but its diameter is much smaller compared to other large trees.

  • Height - 115.7m
  • Diameter - 4.8m
  • Species - Sequoia sempervirens
  • Location - Redwoods National Park, California, USA

1. General Sherman (Giant Sequoia)

General Sherman is the largest tree on Earth by volume, and it has a massive trunk, making it the widest of the large trees.

  • Height - 83.8
  • Diameter - 11.1
  • Species - Sequoiadendron giganteum
  • Location - Sequoia National Park, California, USA
